Privacy Policy

Plainfully clarity check

No hidden purposes. No “we may” unless we truly must. Just what we do, and why.

What we collect

  • Content you submit for analysis
  • Routing detail needed to deliver your result
  • Basic operational logs (limited duration)

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run ad profiling based on submissions.
  • We do not build behavioural profiles about individuals.

Why we process data

  • To provide clarification/scam checking you requested
  • To maintain reliability and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., billing records)
