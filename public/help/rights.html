Your Rights (GDPR)

Plainfully clarity check

GDPR rights translated into practical actions you can actually take.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy
  • Rectification — correct inaccuracies
  • Erasure — request deletion where applicable
  • Restriction — limit certain processing
  • Objection — object to certain lawful bases
  • Portability — receive data in a portable format where applicable

How to request

Use the support channel shown on the site. We may verify ownership before actioning a request.

Note: Some records (e.g., billing) may be retained for legal compliance.
