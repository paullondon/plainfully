Cookies & Analytics

Plainfully clarity check

If we don’t need a cookie, we don’t use it. If analytics are enabled, they must be explainable and optional.

Cookies Plainfully may use

Plainfully aims to use the smallest cookie footprint possible.

Essential cookies

These are used to keep you signed in and to maintain basic site security.

Analytics cookies (if enabled)

If analytics are enabled, they are used to understand broad usage (for example, page popularity) and to improve the service. We do not use analytics to create advertising profiles.

Unknown / optional: If you are unsure what is enabled on your deployment, treat analytics as optional and disable until confirmed.

How to control cookies

  • You can block cookies in your browser settings.
  • For analytics, you may also be able to opt out via an in-site toggle (if implemented).
