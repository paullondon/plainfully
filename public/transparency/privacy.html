Privacy Policy

Plainfully clarity check

This policy states what we collect, what we do not collect, and why — without hiding behind fuzzy wording.

What this policy covers

This policy explains how Plainfully collects and uses information when you submit content for analysis.

What we collect

  • Content you submit (the message/email text)
  • Your contact/routing detail so we can deliver your result (for example, your email address)
  • Basic operational logs (timestamps, delivery status, internal reference ID)

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not run ads based on your submissions.
  • We do not build behavioural profiles about you.

Why we use data

  • To provide the clarification / scam-check service you asked for
  • To maintain service reliability and prevent abuse
  • To meet legal obligations (for example, billing records where applicable)

Who we share data with

We only share data with service providers required to operate Plainfully (for example, email delivery or hosting), and only to the extent needed to provide the service.

How long we keep data

See Data Usage & Retention for exact retention windows.

Contact

If you have a privacy concern, contact us using the support channel shown on the site.
