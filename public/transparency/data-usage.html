Data Usage & Retention

Plainfully clarity check

This page explains where your data goes, why it exists, and when it is deleted — in plain steps.

How data flows through Plainfully

  1. You submit content (email, text, or website form).
  2. Plainfully processes it to produce a result.
  3. The result is returned to you and shown on your dashboard.
  4. The original content is deleted according to retention rules.

Retention principles

  • Keep data only as long as it is useful to the user.
  • Delete by default, not by exception.
  • Separate billing records from message content.

Typical retention (high level)

Data type Why it exists Retention
Submitted content (message text) Generate the result Short fixed window, then deleted
Result summary Show on dashboard While your account is active (or until deleted)
Operational logs Reliability / abuse prevention Limited duration
Billing records (if paid) Legal / accounting Retained as required by law
Note: Exact retention values depend on your current product configuration and may be shown in-app.
