Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

Plainfully clarity check

This page shows what risks exist when processing your content — and exactly what controls reduce those risks.

1. Purpose of this assessment

Plainfully processes limited personal data to provide clarification analysis on user-submitted content (for example, an email or message you ask us to explain). This DPIA records the privacy risks of that processing and the controls we apply to reduce those risks.

2. What data is processed

  • Content you submit for analysis (text/body)
  • Routing details required to deliver the result (for example, the replying email address)
  • Operational metadata (timestamps, internal reference IDs, and delivery status)
We do not aim to collect: profiling data, contact lists, behavioural tracking, or unrelated identifiers.

3. Lawful basis

  • Consent: you choose to submit content to be analysed.
  • Legitimate interests: delivering the clarification service you requested.

4. Data minimisation controls

  • We process the minimum text required to generate a helpful result.
  • Attachments are restricted by allowlist and are rejected when not needed.
  • We avoid storing duplicate copies of the same content.

5. Retention and deletion

Plainfully is designed to keep user content for the shortest time that still makes the service usable. Data is deleted automatically according to our Data Usage & Retention rules.

6. AI and automation safeguards

  • AI outputs are advisory and should not be treated as legal, medical, or financial advice.
  • User content is processed only for the requested purpose (clarification / scam check).
  • We do not use user submissions to build public profiles about individuals.

7. Security measures

  • Access control (least privilege) and audit-friendly logging
  • Encryption in transit; encryption at rest where supported
  • Separation of web access from internal processing systems

8. Risk summary

Risk Impact Control Residual risk
Over-retention of content Unnecessary exposure over time Automatic deletion + limited retention windows Low
Over-collection Processing unnecessary data Input constraints + allowlists Low
Data leakage Unauthorised access Access control + encryption + separation Low
Misinterpretation of AI output User confusion / wrong action Clear disclaimers + plain-language summaries Medium → Managed

9. Outcome

With the controls above, the overall residual risk is assessed as low, and is reviewed if the service changes.
