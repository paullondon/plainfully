AI Processing & Automation

Plainfully clarity check

This explains what automation does (and does not do) so you can trust the boundaries, not just the branding.

What AI does here

Plainfully uses automation to summarise, clarify, and highlight risks in the content you submit.

What AI does not do

  • It does not make decisions that have legal or similarly significant effects on you.
  • It does not replace professional advice.
  • It does not grant access to your accounts or act on your behalf.

Safeguards

  • Purpose-limited processing: only to generate the result you requested.
  • Clear disclaimers: outputs are guidance, not guarantees.
  • Abuse controls: limits help prevent malicious use.

Human control

You control what is submitted. If you do not want content processed, do not submit it.
