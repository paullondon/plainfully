Data & Privacy FAQ

Plainfully clarity check

This FAQ links to the exact page that answers each question, so nothing is buried in legal pages.

Quick answers

Does Plainfully track me?

Plainfully aims not to profile users. If analytics are enabled, they are used for broad service improvement only. See Cookies & Analytics.

Is my content used to train AI?

Plainfully processes what you submit to generate your result. We do not aim to use your submissions to build public profiles about you. See AI Processing & Automation.

How long does my data exist?

Plainfully keeps data only as long as it is useful and lawful. See Data Usage & Retention.

Can I delete my data?

In many cases, yes. Some records may be retained for legal reasons (e.g., billing). See Your Rights.

Why is this written differently from “normal” policy pages?

Because unclear wording defeats the point. These pages are designed to be specific, testable, and minimal.
